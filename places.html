Top Places to Visit in Thiruvananthapuram

Padmanabhaswamy Temple

A historic temple known for its intricate architecture and spiritual significance.

Kovalam Beach

Famous for its crescent-shaped coastline, lighthouse, and vibrant beach activities.

Ponmudi Hills

A serene hill station offering scenic views, trekking trails, and lush greenery.


Top Places to Visit in Kollam

Ashtamudi Lake

Known for its picturesque backwaters, houseboat cruises, and serene ambiance.

Jatayu Earth's Center

A unique rock theme park with the world's largest bird sculpture and adventure activities.

Palaruvi Waterfalls

A stunning waterfall nestled in the forest, ideal for picnics and nature walks.


Top Places to Visit in Pathanamthitta

Sabarimala Temple

A major pilgrimage center dedicated to Lord Ayyappa, attracting millions of devotees.

Gavi

A beautiful eco-tourism spot offering wildlife, trekking, and boating experiences.

Konni Elephant Training Center

A historic elephant training center where visitors can interact with elephants.


Top Places to Visit in Alappuzha (Alleppey)

Alleppey Backwaters

Renowned for houseboat cruises through the scenic backwaters and paddy fields.

Marari Beach

A tranquil beach known for its clean sands and peaceful ambiance.

Krishnapuram Palace

A historic palace featuring Kerala-style architecture and ancient murals.


Top Places to Visit in Kottayam

Kumarakom Backwaters

Famous for bird watching, houseboat cruises, and lush landscapes.

Vaikom Mahadeva Temple

One of the oldest temples in Kerala, dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Illikkal Kallu

A popular trekking destination with stunning rock formations and panoramic views.


Top Places to Visit in Idukki

Munnar

Known for its tea plantations, cool climate, and picturesque landscapes.

Thekkady

Home to Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, famous for its elephants and boat rides.

Idukki Dam

One of the highest arch dams in Asia, offering breathtaking views and boating.


Top Places to Visit in Ernakulam (Kochi)

Fort Kochi

Known for its colonial architecture, Chinese fishing nets, and vibrant arts scene.

Marine Drive

A scenic waterfront promenade with beautiful views of the backwaters and the sunset.

Mattancherry Palace

Also known as the Dutch Palace, showcasing Kerala murals and artifacts.


Top Places to Visit in Thrissur

Athirappilly Waterfalls

Often called the "Niagara of India," famous for its breathtaking cascades.

Vadakkunnathan Temple

An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its cultural significance.

Thrissur Pooram

A grand festival featuring elaborate elephant processions and traditional music.


Top Places to Visit in Palakkad

Silent Valley National Park

A pristine rainforest reserve with rich biodiversity and scenic beauty.

Palakkad Fort

A historic fort with well-preserved architecture and a beautiful garden.

Malampuzha Dam and Gardens

A popular spot for picnics, boating, and enjoying the scenic landscape.


Top Places to Visit in Malappuram

Kottakkunnu

Known as the 'Ooty of Malabar,' offering hilltop views, gardens, and an amphitheater.

Nilambur Teak Museum

The world's first teak museum, showcasing the history and beauty of teak wood.

Thirunavaya Temple

An ancient temple by the Bharathapuzha River, significant for Hindu rituals.


Top Places to Visit in Kozhikode (Calicut)

Kappad Beach

The historic beach where Vasco da Gama landed in 1498.

Beypore

Renowned for its shipbuilding yard and scenic views of the Chaliyar River.

Mananchira Square

A popular park with a musical fountain, surrounded by historical landmarks.


Top Places to Visit in Wayanad

Edakkal Caves

Ancient caves with prehistoric carvings, offering a glimpse into early human life.

Chembra Peak

The highest peak in Wayanad, known for its heart-shaped lake and trekking trails.

Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

A sanctuary rich in wildlife, including elephants, tigers, and exotic birds.


Top Places to Visit in Kannur

Muzhappilangad Drive-in Beach

India's only drive-in beach, known for its long stretch of sandy shores.

St. Angelo Fort

A historic fort offering panoramic views of the Arabian Sea.

Payyambalam Beach

A popular beach with beautiful sands, gardens, and a relaxed vibe.


Top Places to Visit in Kasaragod

Bekal Fort

A massive fort by the sea, known for its scenic views and historical significance.

Chandragiri Fort

An ancient fort overlooking the confluence of the Chandragiri River and the Arabian Sea.

Ananthapura Lake Temple

A unique temple situated in the middle of a lake, known for its serene setting.
